About Me

After working for a non-profit organization focusing on HIV/AIDS prevention and intervention in China, I came to the United States, and obtained a Master’s Degree in Anthropology at Purdue University.


I found my passion in coding and data analysis, while I was taking classes in Statistics, and analyzing data at Purdue. I spent hours and hours on class projects in SAS, and didn’t feel any fatigue. Instead, I felt fulfilled and excited. That's why I decided to pursue a career in data science.


I finished eight-week web development program of HTML, CSS, JavaScript in MotherCoders in fall 2016. I am learned jQuery, Python through various online resourses such as Codecademy, Free Code Camp, Coursera.


I am taking six-month software engineering program at Techtonica, focusing on algorithm, data structures, and computer science fundamentals. I built full-stack web applications with JavaScript, SQL, and the MERN stack: MongoDB, Express, React, Node.
